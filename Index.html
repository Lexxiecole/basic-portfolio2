About Ace Elizabeth Designs

Ace Elizabeth Designs was founded in 2017 by Alexandra Cole. After spending a large amount of time painting water color, she decided to design a Bachelorette Party invitation for a friend. After that, others invited her to make invitations.

All artwork featured on Ace Elizabeth Designs is the original work of Alexandra, ranging from painting watercolors, to photography, to renderings on her iPad.

Coming soon will be photograph editing as well as the sale of hand made pottery.
